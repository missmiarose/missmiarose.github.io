Skank History

Skank History is an unapologetically bold and hilariously honest docu-series that dives into the wild, absurd, and all-too-human side of sex work. We sit down with former escorts who share their most unforgettable — and often side-splitting — stories from the job. Then, we bring those tales to life with re-enactments performed by real adult film stars.

Equal parts comedy, confession, and cultural commentary, Skank History turns taboo into truth-telling, reminding audiences that behind every scandalous story is a real person with a sharp wit, a beating heart, and a sense of humor about it all.
