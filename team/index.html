Redefining the Future of Film

Mia Rose — Owner, Creative Director

Mia Rose went from nothing to becoming a trailblazer, pioneer, and record-breaker. It's a story that begins in the gutter and culminates with Rose becoming associated with names like Howard Stern, James Gunn, and even Stan Lee. As a child, she faced a seemingly insurmountable challenge, but amazingly transformed that into a career filled with awards, surging popularity, and even a highlight in the publication People Australia.
Active nearly 20 years in the adult entertainment sphere, Mia kicked things off with XRCO's Cream Dream of the Year award, then went on to star in 140 standalone titles and enjoy further acclaim with multiple AVN nominations. She remains one of the best-known starlets of the era, and one who enjoys a fiercely loyal fanbase.
But her successes don't stop with adult entertainment: Mia also rose to prominence as a gaming personality. She made history by becoming the first female streamer on Twitch, where she ultimately amassed a whopping 90,000 followers. She was even awarded an NPC in the mega computer game hit, World of Warcraft, which launched her even further into the gaming stratosphere.
Lastly, she discovered and cultivated her love for man's best friend, even acting as a trainer of seeing-eye dogs for several years. And that led to her discovery of a real-life hero in the animal kingdom: the she-wolf that reigned above male wolves for years in Yellowstone, as detailed in a recent documentary. Mia is determined to capture that strength and bravery in her most important project to date, \"Alpha 6,\" a new adult film that will reinforce the fact that females can do anything.

Cleanface — Director/Cinematographer

Operating under his nom de Guerre, Cleanface is a seasoned filmmaker with over a decade of experience in motion picture storytelling. His extensive background encompasses various roles in the mainstream film industry, including cinematographer, editor, director, screenwriter, and producer. In 2025, JPC established Royal Imperial Pictures to embark on a journey of creative expression in the adult film industry. His accomplishments in film and television are many, but we are under strict orders not to disclose them.

Alex Fuchs - Producer/Writer/Per

Long before becoming an adult film performer Alex was involved in the performing arts. Hestarted his theater career at 4 years old, as Trouble in a production of Madame Butterfly at the Oakland Opera, since then he has worked in stage direction, as a stagehand, as a prop carpenter, and done light and sound rigging for a production company specializing in outdoor stage construction.
Since joining the team at Mia Rose he has performed, been responsible for props and other practical effects, created the concept for one of our short form projects, and is co-writing our upcoming feature, 'Sister Sin', along with Cleanface.
